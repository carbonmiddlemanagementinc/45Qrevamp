Methodology: "Bankable" is estimated by comparing the tier credit against each sector's published capture cost distribution. Conservative count = credit exceeds the upper end of the cost range (most facilities viable). High count = credit exceeds the lower end (some facilities viable). Actual deployment depends on permitting, CO₂ transport and storage infrastructure, Class VI well availability, and project financing — all constraints independent of credit level. DAC facilities are drawn from the IEA CCUS Projects Database U.S. pipeline rather than Rhodium, which did not cover DAC. Coal and gas power are excluded from this analysis as they map to Tier A but face additional regulatory and market headwinds beyond cost alone.
